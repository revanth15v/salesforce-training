# Salesforce Basics

## 1. What is CRM?

CRM stands for **Customer Relationship Management**.
It is a system used to manage:
- Customer details
- Communication
- Sales
- Customer support 


---

## 2. Why Companies Use Salesforce?

Companies use Salesforce to:

- Store customer data
- Track sales and leads
- Improve customer service
- Automate business processes
- Generate reports and analytics

---

# 3. Salesforce Objects

## Account
An **Account** represents a company or organization in Salesforce.

### Examples
- Infosys
- Amazon

---

## Contact
A **Contact** is a person associated with an Account.

It stores details such as:
- Name
- Email
- Phone Number

### Example
An employee or customer working in a company.

---

## Opportunity
An **Opportunity** represents a potential business deal or sale.
It helps companies:
- Track sales progress
- Manage deals
- Estimate expected revenue

---

# 4. Real-World Mapping

## College Admission Example

| Salesforce Object | Real-World Example |
|-------------------|-------------------|
| Account | College |
| Contact | Student applying for admission |
| Lead | Student showing interest in joining the college |
| Opportunity | Student admission for a specific course |
